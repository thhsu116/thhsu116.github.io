Starting from 1998, NBA tracks the location and distance to the rim of every field goal attempt (FGA) in a game.
  This project shows, on average:
	1. How a NBA team's FGA is distributed over different distances in a game 
	2. How FGA distribution evolves over time

Single Season FGA Distribution

  FGA is categorized according to its distance to the rim, and each category is associated 
  with a number representing the portion of total FGA comming from the categoty. Use the 
  dropdown menu to select year. Notice the drastic change in FGA distribution from 
  year 1998 to 2023.

Chane of FGA Distribution Over Time

  Click on the legend to show/hide how the portion of total FGA of a category changes over time.
  Notice the upwoard trend of 3 point shots and the downward trend of long two point shots ( 
  shots from 16 feet beyond but within 3 point line)
